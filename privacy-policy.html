Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2023

Welcome to Image Converter Pro. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our image conversion service, we may collect the following information:

  • Images you upload for conversion (processed entirely in your browser)
  • Technical information about your device and browser
  • Usage data including timestamps and selected conversion options

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our service

3. Data Security

All image processing occurs directly in your browser. We do not store your images on our servers. Your images are never transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly choose to save them to our cloud storage (premium feature).

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics to help analyze how users use our site. These services may use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your use of our website.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date.
